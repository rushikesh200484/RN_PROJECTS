About RN Projects

RN Projects is a student-led initiative by Rushikesh Nanavare to provide ready-made as well as custom-engineered solutions for students in Electrical and Electronics domains. We focus on innovative, low-cost, and technically rich projects using Arduino and other microcontroller platforms.
